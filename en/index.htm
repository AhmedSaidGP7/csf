About The Event

This event is an event that designed to educate new university students in many areas that may help them in their life after graduation, specifically in graphic design, student activities and their importance, and career life after graduation. This event takes place at Suez University and it is just an introduction to previous topics.

Graphic Design

In this session, we will talk about graphic design, we will give you essential knowledge about graphic design, how to be a professional graphic designer, and what you should study to become an effective graphic designer.

Student Activities

In this session, we will talk about the concept of student activities, what is that, what is its importance, how that will help you in your life after graduation, and how this will change your life amazingly!

Career life

In this session, the President of the CCT Foundation will talk about many important career-related topics, which will help you qualify yourself in your study period to ensure getting a suitable job after graduation.

Marketing 101

In this session, the IEEE Chairman will talk about the principles of marketing. what is that, what its job, why it is important, and what you have to study in order to become a professional marketer.
